About Us

ChildrenCare is a community-driven initiative led by PS Tushar, dedicated to improving the lives of children through health, education, and nutrition programs. By leveraging video proceeds and community support, we organize regular food drives, distribute school kits, and provide essential healthcare services, ensuring every child has the opportunity to thrive.
